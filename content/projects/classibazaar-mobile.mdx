# Welcome to ClassiBazaar

ClassiBazaar is a one-stop solution for online classifieds. Whether you're
looking to buy, sell, or rent, we make the process easy, secure, and efficient.

---

## What We Offer

### **For Sellers**

- Post your ads quickly with our user-friendly interface.
- Reach thousands of potential buyers instantly.
- Manage your listings with real-time updates.

### **For Buyers**

- Explore a wide range of categories, from electronics to real estate.
- Use advanced filters to find exactly what you need.
- Connect with verified sellers for a seamless transaction.

```jsx
<Highlight>
  ClassiBazaar connects communities by making local commerce accessible to
  everyone.
</Highlight>
```

---

## Key Features

1. **Secure Transactions**  
   With integrated payment options, transactions are safe and reliable.

2. **Advanced Search Options**  
   Save time by filtering by location, price range, and category.

3. **Mobile-Friendly**  
   Access our platform on the go with a fully responsive design.

## Download the ClassiBazaar App

Experience the convenience of ClassiBazaar on your mobile device! Follow the
guide below to download our app:

### **For iOS Users**

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Search for **ClassiBazaar** in the search bar.
3. Tap the **Download** button to install the app.
4. Open the app, sign in, and start exploring!

[![Download on the App Store](https://cdn-icons-png.flaticon.com/128/16566/16566128.png)](https://apps.apple.com/au/app/classibazaar/id1496587117)

---

### **For Android Users**

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device.
2. Search for **ClassiBazaar** in the search bar.
3. Tap the **Install** button to download the app.
4. Open the app, sign in, and start exploring!
