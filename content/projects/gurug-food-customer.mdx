# Welcome to Gurug Food

Gurug Food is your go-to app for a seamless food delivery experience. Whether
you're craving fast food, home-cooked meals, or something new, Gurug Food
connects you with local restaurants for fast, easy, and reliable deliveries.
Available on both the
[Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.beccorestaurant&hl=en_CA)
and the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gurug-food/id1585408458),
Gurug Food makes dining at home more enjoyable than ever.

---

## Why Choose Gurug Food?

### 1. Wide Variety of Restaurants

From fast food to gourmet meals, Gurug Food offers a diverse range of cuisines
from local restaurants.

### 2. Fast and Reliable Delivery

Order and get your food delivered quickly, with reliable delivery tracking and
updates.

### 3. User-Friendly Interface

Browse menus, customize your order, and track deliveries easily with our
intuitive app design.

### 4. Exclusive Offers

Get access to exclusive discounts, promotions, and deals that make ordering your
favorite food even better.

---

## Key Features

- **Explore Local Restaurants**: Discover a wide variety of local restaurants
  and cuisines right at your fingertips.
- **Easy Order Customization**: Customize your meal just the way you like it and
  place your order in a few taps.
- **Track Your Delivery**: Get real-time updates on your order status with
  detailed tracking.
- **Secure Payments**: Enjoy multiple payment options, including **Khalti** for
  digital payments and **Cash on Delivery** for added convenience.
- **Save Your Favorites**: Keep track of your favorite restaurants and meals for
  easy access on your next order.

## How It Works

1. **Download the App**  
   Get Gurug Food from the
   [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.beccorestaurant&hl=en_CA)
   or [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gurug-food/id1585408458).

2. **Browse Menus and Place Your Order**  
   Browse through a variety of restaurants and menu items, customize your order,
   and place it easily.

3. **Select Your Payment Method**  
   Choose between **Khalti**, a secure digital wallet for instant payments, or
   **Cash on Delivery** for convenience at the time of delivery.

4. **Track Your Delivery**  
   Follow your order with real-time tracking and get notified when your food is
   on the way.

5. **Enjoy Your Meal**  
   Enjoy delicious food delivered right to your door, hot and fresh!
