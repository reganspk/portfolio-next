# Welcome to Gurug Food Merchant

Gurug Food Merchant is a comprehensive platform designed to help restaurant
owners and food vendors manage their orders and deliveries efficiently.
Available on the
[App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/guru-g-food-merchant/id1595532192),
Gurug Food Merchant streamlines your operations and helps you grow your food
business by reaching a larger customer base.

---

## Why Choose Gurug Food Merchant?

### 1. Effortless Order Management

Manage incoming orders with ease, view order details, and track delivery status
in real-time.

### 2. Simplified Payment Processing

Accept payments securely through multiple methods, including **Khalti** and
**Cash on Delivery**.

### 3. Real-Time Notifications

Stay updated with instant notifications for new orders, cancellations, and
status changes.

### 4. Detailed Analytics

Get insights into your sales, popular items, and customer preferences to make
data-driven decisions.

---

## Key Features

- **Order Management**: View and manage orders in real-time with a simple and
  intuitive interface.
- **Payment Integration**: Accept payments securely via **Khalti** and **Cash on
  Delivery** options.
- **Delivery Tracking**: Monitor the progress of your deliveries and ensure
  on-time service.
- **Sales Analytics**: Access detailed reports on your sales, revenue, and order
  history.
- **Easy Menu Updates**: Update your menu items, prices, and availability
  effortlessly.
- **Customer Interaction**: Communicate with customers directly to resolve
  queries and ensure satisfaction.

## How It Works

1. **Download the App**  
   Get Gurug Food Merchant from the
   [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/guru-g-food-merchant/id1595532192).

2. **Set Up Your Restaurant Profile**  
   Add your restaurant details, menu items, prices, and operational hours to get
   started.

3. **Receive and Manage Orders**  
   Accept and process incoming orders, update order statuses, and manage
   deliveries in real-time.

4. **Get Paid Securely**  
   Process payments through **Khalti** or **Cash on Delivery**, ensuring your
   transactions are safe and reliable.

5. **Track Your Performance**  
   Review your sales, track popular items, and gain insights to improve your
   offerings.
