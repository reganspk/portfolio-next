## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Dashboard**: Access all your events, ticket sales, and
  attendee data from a single dashboard.
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Monitor ticket sales, attendance, and revenue in real
  time.
- **Secure Payment Processing**: Integrate with trusted payment systems to
  handle transactions securely.
- **Attendee Management**: Easily track and manage your attendees with digital
  check-ins and QR code scanning.
- **Customizable Options**: Set ticket prices, sales limits, and event details
  effortlessly.
