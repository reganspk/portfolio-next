# Welcome to ClassiBazaar

ClassiBazaar is Australia's premier online marketplace, connecting buyers and
sellers with a secure and user-friendly platform. Whether you're looking for
great deals, unique items, or local services, ClassiBazaar offers a seamless
experience with innovative features like a real-time chat system and secure
payments powered by Stripe.

---

## Why Choose ClassiBazaar?

### 1. Secure and Convenient Transactions

With our Stripe-powered payment system, you can trade with confidence knowing
your transactions are safe and secure.

### 2. Real-Time Communication

Our integrated chat system makes it easy to connect with buyers or sellers
instantly, discuss details, and finalize your deal.

### 3. Diverse Listings

From electronics to real estate, cars to services, you’ll find everything you
need in one place.

### 4. Trusted and Local

Join a growing community of verified users and explore listings close to you.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Chat System**: Communicate directly with buyers and sellers
  through a built-in chat feature to streamline negotiations.
- **Secure Payments with Stripe**: Enjoy peace of mind with our Stripe-powered
  payment system, ensuring fast, reliable, and secure transactions.
- **Extensive Categories**: Browse through thousands of listings across various
  categories tailored to your needs.
- **Advanced Search Options**: Filter by location, price, or category to quickly
  find what you're looking for.
- **Mobile-Friendly Design**: Access ClassiBazaar anytime, anywhere, on any
  device.

## How It Works

1. **Browse Listings**  
   Navigate through categories to discover items, services, and opportunities
   near you.

2. **Chat Instantly**  
   Use our chat system to connect directly with sellers or buyers, ask
   questions, and arrange deals.

3. **Pay Securely**  
   Complete your transactions with Stripe's secure payment gateway for a
   hassle-free experience.

4. **Enjoy Your Purchase**  
   Pick up or have your items delivered, knowing you’ve traded safely.
