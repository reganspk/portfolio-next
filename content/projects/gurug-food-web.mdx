# Welcome to Gurug Food Customer Web

Gurug Food Customer Web is the ultimate online platform for food lovers. Order
food from a wide range of restaurants and get it delivered right to your door.
Available on [Gurug Food](https://www.gurugfood.com), the web-based platform
makes it easy to explore local eateries, browse menus, and place orders, all
from the comfort of your home.

---

## Why Choose Gurug Food Customer Web?

### 1. Wide Restaurant Selection

Choose from a variety of local restaurants offering different cuisines, ensuring
there's something for everyone.

### 2. Easy Online Ordering

Browse through menus, select your favorite dishes, and place your order with
just a few clicks.

### 3. Secure Payment Options

Pay for your meals securely with multiple payment methods, including **Khalti**
and **Cash on Delivery**.

### 4. Real-Time Order Tracking

Track your order status in real-time and get live updates on delivery progress.

## Key Features

- **Restaurant Selection**: Browse through a wide range of restaurants in your
  area and find the best options.
- **Online Ordering**: Easily order food online from your favorite eateries
  without hassle.
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Pay through **Khalti**, **Cash on Delivery**, or

- **Order History**: View your past orders, reorder your favorite meals, and
  keep track of your spending.

---

## How It Works

1. **Visit the Website**  
   Go to [Gurug Food Customer Web](https://www.gurugfood.com) on your browser.

2. **Explore the Menu**  
   Browse through the list of available restaurants and explore their menus.

3. **Place Your Order**  
   Select the dishes you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

4. **Choose Payment Option**  
   Pay using your preferred method—**Khalti**, **Cash on Delivery**, or other
   secure options.

5. **Track Your Order**  
   Once your order is placed, track its progress in real-time until it arrives
   at your doorstep.
